Marrakech - Taroudante

5 days / 4 nights

1 day: Marrakech â€“ Ait ben hadou - Ouarzazate

Departure from your hotel in the morning for to cross the tishka pass in the high atlas mountains .along the way we will stop by many Berber villages . fields that steak to the mountains before that we will get a side road to head to telouet for a Visit to the local Kasbah that was once the main residence of el pasha el glaoui the leader of all the south of morocco during the French occupation . from there we continue via the valley of ounila for to get to the Kasbah of ait ben hadou .that is classified as a unesco world heritage site . after the visit and lunch we get to ouarzazate where we spend the night.

2 day: Ouarzazate - Echigaga

Today we start for to cross the mountains of tinifift. Then agdez where start the Sceneries of draa valley with thousands of palm trees and many clay villages. Ksours along this oasis. we will stop by many of these landscapes for a hike Or to take pictures. Lunch in zagora. then head to mhamid .the last village before desert .we will have a camel Ride for 1 hour or 2 hours in the dunes of erg lihoudi and after that the lunch We will cross almost 60 km of the Hamada desert stones and a holly oasis For to get to the dunes of echigaga which increases the height of 300 meter And extends for 35 km .after the sunset on the dunes .dinner and night in a tent.

3 day: Echigaga - Taroudante

We leave chegaga dunes passing trough of rocky desert and iriqui lake which is considered to be a point for different flocks of birds fly to . before arriving to foum zgid and stop for lunch break . the trip will continue to visit taznekht which is known for it s Berber handmade carpets .then we will head for taroudant passing by the village of Taliouine. We shall arrive at afternoon.

4 day: Taroudant - Essaouira

After breakfast. we will start exploring the city of taroudant and it s historical ramparts then we will move to essaouira trough the valley of souss where argan trees line up Till agadir. Before setting to essaouira we will pass by the Valley of paradise and taghazoute .after arriving to essaouira we will discover the old medina .sekala .portugaise harbour and the beach.

5 day: Essaouira - Marrakech

In morning we will continue the discovery the city then we will leave to Marrakech Across the association of argan oil production which is known universally by it s aesthetic skin lotion . Eventually getting to Marrakech in the afternoon.
