The imperial cities & the desert

9 days / 8 nights

1 day: Casablanca

After your arrival, you ll be met and transferred to your hotel. the sightseeing of Casablanca depends on your arrival time . the program of sightseeing includes the Mosque of Hassan II , a masterpiece of modern and traditional architecture in morocco and it is Considered to be one of the biggest mosques in the world.

2 day: Casablanca - Rabat

After the breakfast we will head for the Atlantic city of Rabat the political capital Of Morocco, where we shall see Chellah the Necropolises Merinde, and Hassan square. Oudaya Kasbah in the afternoon. Check in your hotel , Have dinner and spend the night.

3 day: Rabat - Meekness - Fez

In the morning of the fourth day we will leave to Meekness . we will stop in Volubilis to visit the remnants of the Roman civilisation before 2000 year. We ll Continue the trip to Moulay Idriss Zerhoun. by lunch time. We will be arriving To meekness where we will get a guided tour to see Mansour gateway (Bab Mansour) And Hedim square (Place Hedim). And then we will take our way to fez in the afternoon.

4 day: Full day in Fez

This day is devoted to a guided visit to the most important monuments in the city Which is a sort of puzzle with it s narrow streets and markets where the smell of spices and herbs is irresistible .Moreover the city is known with it s miscellaneous Handicrafts made from pottery .leather. And copper. The day will also be devoted to Explore the Medersas religious Islamic universities Abou Anania , Nejarine fountains And finally karaouine mosque.

5 day: Fez - Ifrane - Midelt - Merzouga

We will leave Fez in the morning to the desert .On our way we will pass by the city Of Ifrane known as Switzerland of morocco because of it s architecture and snowed Mountains which attract many skiers. the trip will continue to Azrou city .the business Centre of the Berber tribes who live around the atlas mountains . They sell their products and such as Berber carpets. And handicraft of oak wood , we will stop in Midelt. To have lunch and simultaneously take a break , after we get some rest carry on our way to get to the desert by afternoon so that we can catch a breathtaking sunset . We will spend the night either in a nomadic tent or a room in a hotel.

6 day: Merzouga - Todra Canyons - Roses valley - Ouarzazate

After the spectacular sunrise we will start our journey again to Todraa canyons On the way there is really an interesting typical style of life which is illustrated In the clothes of local people and building architecture . We shall stop by the Oasis of Todraa Valley before getting to the canyons where we can have a hike and then We will head for Ouarzazate. in the way there will be some stops firstly at the town Of kelaat Mgouna also known as the capital of roses where an annual festival takes place . the festival is great chance for locals to display their products made from roses such as soap .perfumes etc .. secondly we ll stop by another small town named Skoura where we will visit its remarkable Kasbah of Amridil . by the end of the day , we shall be in Ouarzazate.

7 day: Full guided day in Marrakech

The whole day will be devoted to visiting the most significant sites with your guide. Marrakech is known as the pearl of the south of morocco . The highlights include the Place of Bahia and Essadiine tombs , after the lunch we will continue our exploration to View more sites such as koutoubia Mosque and the souks of handicraft .we will end The day up by a viewing other sites like jamaa el Fna square known with it s snake Charmers , Stories tellers , Youth with chained musician . Barbary apes

8 day: Marrakech - Casablanca

In the morning of this day you ll be free until 2 pm and it will be up to you to spend the time shopping , Strolling around , Relaxing . After lunch we ll pack up to head for Casablanca to spend the last day of the trip.

9 day: Transfer to the airport

Transfer to the airoport, and wish to see you an other time again, in a new trip around Morocco.
