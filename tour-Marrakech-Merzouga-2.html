Marrakech – Merzouga

3 days / 2 nights

1 day: Marrakech - rose’s valley - dadess valley

We will start in the morning from your hotel into Marrakech Through tizzi N tichka 2260 m in the high Atlas Mountains. along the way the scenery Of the Berber fields and villages that steak to the mountains don t stop .We will have many stops along all these places .lunch in ouarzazate For to continue the trip bye skoura oasis here will visit the amredil Kasbah .then pass bye the roses valley and dadess valley before to Get to dadess gorge . Hike here and spend the night in a hotel here.

2 day : dadess - todra gorges - Merzouga

After the breakfast .starting to todra gorges .before that we will Get there you ll cross the small town of tinghir and the valley Of of todra .lunch there and after the a break for visiting we Drive to the dunes of erg echabi in merzouga .after a mint Tea there go for a camel ride for to reach the the oasis after That we ll cross 5 km of sand dunes. Spend the night in a Berber tent.

3 day: Merzouga – Marrakech

Sunrise on the dunes then breakfast. We leave the desert among the Desertic scenaries in the Mountains of saghro. For to get to the Oasis of nekoub .lunch here in restaurant that offer a view On the village and the oasis .we continue via the historical Valley of draa with it s huge palm grove oasis and many Ksours and Kasbahs. Before that we ll get to agdez. From There we ll get to ouarzazate.And continue via the Atlas Mountains For to get to marakech in the afternoon.
