1 day trip to Ouzoud waterfall

In the morning, the trip will set off toward Ouzoud waterfalls, which is 110 m altitude. This waterfalls is considered as one of the most attractive sightseeing in Morocco. Once arrived, we will go on a trekking tour to explore the surroundings of the waterfalls, and in the evening we will travel back to Marrakech.
