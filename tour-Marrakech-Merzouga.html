The pearls of the south

3 days / 2 nights

1 day: Marrakech - Skoura - Roses valley- Dadess canyons

We'll start in the morning from your hotel to Dadess valley, trough Tizzi Tichka (2260 m. high) in atlas mountains, nearby the scenaries of Berber villages in the foothhills of the mountains with many stops along the road and its wonderful landscapes, lunch in Ouarzazate, then continue to Skoura having a hike in the oasis and visiting the Kasbah of Amredil, then pass by the Rosés valley and Dadess canyons by the end of the sunset spending the night in a local hotel.

2 day: Dadess canyons - Todra valley - Merzouga

After breakfast we'll head to Todra canyons valley with many stops at different scenarios in these canyons. We'll have a break for lunch and a hike then continue to Erfoud Erissani town and Erg Echabi dunes in Merzouga. In the afternoon, we'll have a camel ride for 2 hours till the middle of the dunes, and the night will be spent in a nomadic tent.

3 day: Merzouga - Draa valley - Marrakech

After the breakfast with the spectacular sunrise on the dunes, we'll get back to Ouarzazate through Tazarine, in our way we'll pass by the desert with amazing landscapes like dry mountains and highlands. We'll visit Berber village's ruins, to get to Nekob village, having lunch there and continue through Draa valley and Agdez where we'll stop in many places with panoramic views. The arrival to Marrakech will be in the afternoon.
