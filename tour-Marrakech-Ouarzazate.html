The desert at a glance

2 days / 1 night

1 day: Marrakech – Ait ben hadou - Ouarzazate – Zagora

Departure from your hotel in the morning for to cross the tishka pass in the high atlas mountains .along the way we will stop by many Berber villages . fields that steak to the mountains before that we will get to the Kasbah of ait ben hadou .that is classified as a unesco world heritage site . After the visit and lunch we continue to cross the mountains of tinifift. Then agdez where start the Sceneries of draa valley with Thousands of palm trees and many clay villages. Ksours along this oasis. we will stop by many of these landscapes for a hike and to take pictures. Afternoon arrival to zagora. Camel trekking for sunset dinner and night in a tent.

2 day: Ouarzazate – Marrakech

After the sunrise and breakfast we will leave the desert to Marrakech via the village Of tam grout known for it's local pottery from there we will get to ouarzazate where we stop for lunch and visit taourirt Kasbah. Then we will continue to Marrakech passing by the high atlas mountains .we shall arrive at afternoon
