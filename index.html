Sahara with Locals LLC, private and small group trips to the desert, mountains, authentic cities

Sahara with locals is small local berber team
that organises private tours with 4 x 4 vehicles and camels trekking to the desert, mountains and oasises.

The authentic cities and berber villages, with 1 to 6 participants, in our several trips which are perfectly suitable for either individuals, families, or even for small groups.
