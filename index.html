A new experience

Sahara with Locals LLC, private and small group trips to the desert, mountains, authentic cities

Sahara with locals is small local berber team
that organises private tours with 4 x 4 vehicles and camels trekking to the desert, mountains and oasises.

The authentic cities and berber villages, with 1 to 6 participants, in our several trips which are perfectly suitable for either individuals, families, or even for small groups.

Our services vary from short excursions to long expeditions which last for days, depending on client's desire.

With modern cars and comfortable accomodations, you can start your tour at the airoport, or from your hotel in Casablanca, Marrakech, Agadir, Ouarzazate, or from anywhere in Morocco.

Please contact us for more details about our listed tours below , or if you have an other plan and we ll discuss it with you
